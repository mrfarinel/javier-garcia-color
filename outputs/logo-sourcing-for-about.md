# Original Logo Sourcing for About Page

Goal: use only original or brand-approved client logos in the About page.

## Confirmed official/public sources

- Netflix: official Netflix Brand Site  
  https://brand.netflix.com/en/assets/logos/?RewriteStatus=1&library=true

- Movistar Plus+: official communication download page, including white-on-black versions  
  https://www.movistarplus.es/comunicacion/notas-de-prensa/acceso-descarga-logos-movistar-plus-2023/

- HBO Max: official HBO Max brand guidelines and logo suite  
  https://brand.hbomax.com/logos

- ONCE: official ONCE logo gallery, including white logo file  
  https://www.once.es/comunicacion/galeria-multimedia/logos-once

- El Corte Ingles: official corporate identity download page  
  https://www.elcorteingles.es/informacioncorporativa/es/comunicacion/identidad-corporativa/

- Renfe: official Renfe brand page  
  https://grupo.renfe.com/es/en/discover-renfe/about-us/brand

- Cerveza Imperial: official Imperial website exposes brand imagery and white brand mark assets  
  https://www.imperialbeerusa.com/

## Needs careful confirmation

- Prime Video: use only an official Amazon/Prime Video brand or press asset. Do not use a random SVG from the internet.
- Apple TV+: Apple has strict trademark usage rules for the Apple logo. Prefer an official Apple Services/Apple TV+ lockup if provided, or use a text-only mention instead.
- Mahou: official assets appear to be behind brand/press access. Best source is Mahou San Miguel or a client-provided brand pack.
- Pilsen Ahumada: needs the exact brand/campaign logo from the client or official beer brand source.
- ASISA: the website confirms the brand, but use a press/brand asset or client-supplied vector where possible.
- Central Lechera Asturiana: use a press/brand asset or client-supplied vector where possible.

## Recommendation

For the final About page:

1. Use official SVG/PNG files for the confirmed brands.
2. Keep all logos monochrome white/off-white on the dark background.
3. For brands without a verified downloadable original, temporarily use clean text labels rather than fake logos.
4. Replace text labels as soon as the official files are provided or downloaded from brand-approved sources.
